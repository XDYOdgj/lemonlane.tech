Elevate Your Kitchen with Yumcian's Electric Salt and Pepper Grinder Set

This stylish and efficient set is designed to enhance both your cooking and seasoning experience. With advanced features and user-friendly design, it's the perfect addition to any modern kitchen.

Convenience Meets Precision

Experience the ease of a one-handed electric grinder that delivers precise seasoning control. The Yumcian set features 6 adjustable coarseness settings, letting you finely tune your grind to suit any recipe or cooking style, whether it's a quick stir-fry or a refined gourmet dish.

Its larger 100ml capacity ensures less frequent refills, so you can focus on cooking without interruptions. The transparent anti-pollution base keeps your spices fresh, free from dust, and allows you to easily monitor the amount left for seamless seasoning management.

Modern, Eco-Friendly Design

Enjoy the convenience of a Type-C rechargeable design that eliminates the need for disposable batteries. Not only is it eco-friendly, but it also saves you money in the long run. The built-in LED light illuminates the grinding area, ensuring accurate portion control even in low light conditions.

Whether you're a home cook or a professional chef, this set is ideal for use in kitchens of all sizes. It includes a variety of spices such as black pepper, white pepper, salt, Himalayan salt, flax seeds, and cumin seeds, making it a versatile tool for any meal.
