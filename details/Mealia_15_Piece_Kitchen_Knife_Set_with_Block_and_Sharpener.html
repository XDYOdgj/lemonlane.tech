Mealia 15-Piece Kitchen Knife Set

Elevate your culinary skills with this comprehensive 15-piece knife set designed for both home cooks and professional chefs. The collection includes a range of essential tools, from the 8.5\" chef's knife for chopping and dicing to the 8.1\" bread knife for slicing fresh loaves. A 7.2\" santoku knife offers versatility in everyday cooking, while the 6.1\" slicing and 5\" utility knives are perfect for precision tasks. The six 4.7\" steak knives add elegance to your dining experience, making this set ideal for both meal prep and serving.

Crafted with durable high-carbon stainless steel, each blade maintains a sharp edge through frequent use, ensuring exceptional performance in the kitchen. The ergonomic wooden handles are built to reduce hand fatigue and provide a secure grip, making it easier to work with even during long cooking sessions. Whether you're preparing ingredients or carving meats, this knife set brings a perfect balance of comfort, control, and efficiency to your kitchen.

What sets this set apart is the included knife block. Not just a storage solution, the block is a stylish addition to your kitchen that keeps your blades clean, organized, and ready to use. The built-in sharpening stone ensures easy maintenance, so you can keep your knives sharp without the need for additional tools. Plus, the convenient recipe stand is designed to hold your tablet or phone while you cook, making it easier to follow instructions with precision.
